### Action 3: Build Your FIRST Database (45 minutes)

Open Notion. Let's build something REAL today.

**Step-by-step:**

1. Click "+ New Page" in Notion
2. Title it: "Freelancer Finance OS"
3. Type `/database` and select "Database - Table"
4. Name it: "Income Tracker"
5. Add these columns:
   - `Name` (default, keep it)
   - `Amount` (Number type, show as Dollar)
   - `Date` (Date type)
   - `Client` (Text type)
   - `Status` (Select type: Paid, Pending, Overdue)

6. Add 3 fake entries to test it:
   - Client A, $500, Today, Paid
   - Client B, $1200, Yesterday, Pending
   - Client C, $750, Last week, Paid

**BOOM!** You just built the foundation of your product.

**Total time:** 45 minutes

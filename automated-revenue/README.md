- Refund rate > 10% → Product-market fit issue

## 🆘 Common Obstacles & Solutions

### "I don't know how to build a Notion template"
**Solution:** Watch 3 YouTube tutorials on Notion databases and formulas. Start simple. Your first version doesn't need to be perfect.

### "I'm worried no one will buy it"
**Solution:** Validate before building. Survey 20 freelancers asking "Would you pay $29 for a template that solves X?" If 5+ say yes, you have validation.

### "I don't have time"
**Solution:** You need 20 hours TOTAL over 2-4 weeks. That's:
- 5 hours/week over 4 weeks, OR
- 10 hours/week over 2 weeks
Block calendar time like a doctor's appointment.

### "There's too much competition"
**Solution:** Competition validates demand. Differentiate through:
- Better design
- More specific niche (e.g., "for freelance designers" vs. generic)
- Superior customer support
- AI integration features

### "I'm not technical enough"
**Solution:** Nothing here requires coding. Lemon Squeezy, ConvertKit, Notion are all no-code tools. Follow step-by-step guides.

### "What if I fail?"
**Solution:** Worst case: You spend 20 hours, learn valuable skills, and have a template you can iterate on. Best case: Life-changing passive income. Risk/reward heavily favors action.

## 💰 Revenue Math (Reality Check)

To hit **$5,000/month**:

At $29 average price:
- Need 173 sales/month
- That's 5.7 sales/day
- With 3% conversion rate: Need 190 visitors/day

**How to get traffic:**
- SEO blog posts: 50-100 visitors/day (after 3-6 months)
- Social media: 20-50 visitors/day (consistent posting)
- Paid ads: Unlimited (if ROAS positive)
- Affiliates: Variable (build over time)

**Realistic Timeline:**
